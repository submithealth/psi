Our Staff

Dr. Moazzam Habib, M.D.

Dr. Moazzam Habib completed his medical training at the world renowned Dow Medical University in Karachi, Pakistan. He moved to the United States in 1985 in order to purse a Residency in Internal Medicine at the University of Medicine & Dentistry New Jersey at St. Peters Medical Center. Dr. Habib followed that with a Clinical Fellowship in Diabetes, Metabolism, and Endocrinology at Robert Wood Johnson Medical School in New Jersey. He has been successfully treating patients in private practice in Indianapolis since 1992

Dr. Habib specializes in the treatment of Diabetes Mellitus Type 1 & 2 and related complications, hypertensions, adrenal issues, parathyroid issues, and diseases of the pituitary. Dr. Habib is active in the medical community, and in his free time enjoys playing and performing music, traveling, as well as enjoying time with his family.

Dr. Habib is highly regarded for his warm bed side manner and his ability to connect with his patients. Patients experience this with each visit when he takes time to explain the aspects of the disease a patient may be suffering from, as well as the plan of treatment.

Stephanie Robinson, N.P.

Stephanie Robinson, N.P. completed her undergraduate studies with a Bachelor's Degree in Nursing from Ball State University. She went on to complete her Masters of Science in Nursing from the University of Indianapolis and has been working as a Board Certified Nurse Practitioner since 2007.

Stephanie specializes in the treatment of Diabetes Type 1 & 2, Thyroid conditions, and hypertension. She stays active in the medical community, and in her free time she enjoys time with her family and traveling.

Stephanie is well known for her warmth, bed side manner, and ability to break down and explain a patient's condition which empowers the patient to take control of their health.
